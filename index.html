Who we are

Our Mission is to give best quality services to our clients. We wants to guide our clients in all tax , company related & financial matters.For us offering quality services on time is important. We value our client's time.

Services.

  • Accounting Services ( Book Keeping & Accounting of all type of entities, Designing & reviewing internal controls in accounting systems & Advice on Ind AS & IFRS
  • Auditing Services (Statutory Audit, Stock Audit, Due diligence, Certification Works, Internal Audit & Cuncurrent audit of Banks
  • Preparation of project reports & CMA data for both working Capital & Term Loan
  • Income Tax related matters( Preparation of Income Tax returns, guiding income tax related matters, Transfer Pricing matters, TDS guidance, appearing before Income Tax Authorities

  • Guiding for starting new Business Ventures
